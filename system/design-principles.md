# Design Principles for AI Execution Systems

## 1. Execution > Intelligence

A system that produces clear, actionable output is more valuable than one that produces clever text.

---

## 2. Structure over Style

Outputs should be:
- structured
- inspectable
- comparable over time

---

## 3. Separate Thinking Stages

Do not rely on one-shot outputs.

Use:
- planner
- executor
- evaluator

---

## 4. Make Uncertainty Visible

Every output should include:
- assumptions
- confidence
- missing information

---

## 5. Optimize for Decision Quality

The goal is not to generate text.

The goal is to:
- improve decisions
- reduce ambiguity
- accelerate execution

---

## 6. Human-in-the-Loop by Default

AI should support—not replace—judgment.

---

## 7. Detect Patterns, Not Just Events

The system should:
- identify recurring risks
- detect systemic issues
- surface hidden dependencies
